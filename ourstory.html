Our Story

Founded in 1925 Mapoma Safari has been an ever growing family, we have been running our lodge for decades and have the most experience on the market. Our story begins in the year 1925 when our Founder James Mapoma stumbled upon the land we are currently based on, he saw potential in the area and had a vision to create the most luxiourious informative and fun experience to show case the beauty of Africa and its wildlife and here we are now and that vision has surpassed even his expectations.

Our Trusted Staff

We are proud of many things here at Mapoma Safari but what we are most proud of is our trusted staff. Our staff carry years of knowledge on the area, the different animal behaviours, the best times and locations to spot your favourite wildlife and more. You are safe in their hands here!
